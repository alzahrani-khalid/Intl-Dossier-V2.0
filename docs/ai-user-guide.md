# AI Features User Guide

Feature: 033-AI Brief Generation & Intelligence Layer

## Overview

The GASTAT International Dossier System includes AI-powered features to help you work more efficiently with diplomatic and international relations data.

## Available AI Features

### 1. Brief Generation

Generate AI-powered briefing documents for any dossier.

#### How to Use

1. Navigate to a **Dossier Detail** page
2. Click the **"Generate Brief"** button (sparkles icon)
3. Select brief type:
   - **Meeting Preparation**: For upcoming meetings
   - **Situation Report**: Current status overview
   - **Background**: Historical context
   - **Talking Points**: Key discussion points
4. Optionally add context (meeting date, purpose, attendees)
5. Click **Generate**
6. Wait for AI to create your brief (typically 10-30 seconds)
7. View, edit, or export the generated brief

#### Brief Contents

Each brief includes:

- **Executive Summary**: Key points at a glance
- **Background**: Historical context
- **Current Situation**: Latest developments
- **Talking Points**: Suggested discussion items
- **Recommended Actions**: Suggested next steps
- **Risk Assessment**: Potential concerns
- **Citations**: Sources from the system

### 2. AI Chat

Ask questions in natural language to quickly find information.

#### How to Use

1. Click the **chat icon** (bottom right corner)
2. Type your question in the input box
3. Press **Enter** or click **Send**
4. View the AI response with citations
5. Click citations to navigate to source data

#### Example Questions

- "What commitments do we have with Japan?"
- "Show me recent engagements with Saudi Arabia"
- "What is the background on the trade agreement?"
- "Who are the key contacts for the energy sector?"
- "ما هي الالتزامات المتعلقة باليابان؟" (Arabic supported)

#### Chat Controls

- **Clear**: Start a new conversation
- **Minimize**: Collapse the chat panel
- **Expand**: Make the chat panel larger
- **Close**: Hide the chat

### 3. Entity Link Suggestions

Get AI suggestions for linking intake tickets to relevant entities.

#### How to Use

1. Open an **Intake Ticket** detail page
2. Go to the **Links** tab
3. Click **"Suggest Links"** button
4. Review AI-suggested entities with confidence scores
5. **Approve** suggestions you agree with
6. **Reject** irrelevant suggestions
7. Manually add any missing links

#### Understanding Confidence Scores

- **90-100%** (Green): High confidence - strong match
- **70-89%** (Yellow): Medium confidence - likely relevant
- **50-69%** (Orange): Low confidence - review carefully

## Tips for Best Results

### Brief Generation

- **Provide context**: Adding meeting details improves relevance
- **Choose the right type**: Match brief type to your need
- **Review and edit**: AI-generated content is a starting point
- **Check citations**: Verify sources for accuracy

### AI Chat

- **Be specific**: "Commitments with Japan in 2024" vs "Japan commitments"
- **Use natural language**: Ask questions as you would to a colleague
- **Try Arabic**: Full Arabic language support available
- **Follow citations**: Click links to see full source documents

### Entity Linking

- **Review all suggestions**: Don't auto-approve everything
- **Check confidence**: Lower scores need more scrutiny
- **Add context manually**: AI may miss domain-specific connections

## Language Support

All AI features support:

- **English** (default)
- **Arabic** (العربية)

The system automatically detects your language preference and responds accordingly.

## Troubleshooting

### "Too many requests"

You've hit the rate limit. Wait a moment and try again.

### "AI service unavailable"

Temporary service issue. The system will retry automatically.

### "Feature disabled"

Contact your administrator to enable this feature.

### Slow responses

AI operations can take 10-60 seconds. Please be patient.

### Unexpected results

Try rephrasing your question or providing more context.

## Privacy & Security

- All AI queries are logged for audit purposes
- Sensitive data classification is respected
- No data is shared with external parties without policy approval
- Your conversations are private to your organization

## Feedback

If you encounter issues or have suggestions for improving AI features, please contact your system administrator or the development team.
