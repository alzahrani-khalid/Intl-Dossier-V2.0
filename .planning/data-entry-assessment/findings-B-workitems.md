# Findings â€” Slice B: Work-item data entry (tasks, commitments, intake tickets)

The work-item data-entry surfaces are mostly wired to the **correct** tables/columns
(`tasks.sla_deadline`/`workflow_stage`, `aa_commitments.due_date`/`owner_*`/`tracking_mode`,
`intake_tickets` enums) â€” the standard create paths and discriminators are sound, and the old
`work_item_dossiers` commitment-RLS table mismatch is already fixed (migration `20260605073000`).
But three paths are functionally broken: **after-action commitments can never save** (insert omits
the NOT-NULL `title`), the **task edit dialog desyncs `workflow_stage` from `status`** (done tasks
keep firing SLA), and the **commitment "External" owner option always 500s** (no contact field vs the
`valid_owner` CHECK). Plus a wide band of i18n raw-key leaks (task toasts), swallowed dossier-link
failures, and a silent wrong `tenant_id` on every task.

Schema facts below were live-verified read-only against staging (`pg_constraint`,
`information_schema.columns`); every CRITICAL/HIGH was re-read at its cited source line.
