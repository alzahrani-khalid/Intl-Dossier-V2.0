# v6.4 — Stabilization & Carryover Sweep

## Why this milestone (vs jumping to v7.0)

v6.3 archived cleanly (28/28 plans, 20/20 requirements, audit passed), but the
`v6.3-MILESTONE-AUDIT.md` §7 + `PROJECT.md` "Next Milestone Goals" enumerate a
concrete carryover list that — left to bleed into v7.0 — would erode the
Intelligence Engine's foundation:

1. **DesignV2 → main merge** is unblocked. Until merged, v6.3's quality gates
   (design-token compliance at `error`, `vi.mock("react-i18next")` factory,
   React vendor 285 KB ceiling, SSH-signed phase-base tags) only enforce inside
   the DesignV2 PR context. New v7.0 work landing through normal `main` PRs
   would skip the gates entirely.
2. **271 Tier-C design-token suppressions / 2336 AST nodes** remain under
   `// gsd-design-token-tier-c-allow` waivers. Every v7.0 UI surface (signal
   triage, digest configurator, alert center, multi-dossier correlation
   panels) builds on tokens. Cleaning Tier-C _during_ v7.0 mixes "what's new"
   with "what was deferred" and makes review impossible.
3. **D-19..D-23 Phase 52 deviations** are kanban-adjacent. v7.0's signal
   triage UI is the most likely consumer of the shared `@dnd-kit/core`
   primitive. Mobile touch DnD scope-out, the Phase 39 `kanban-*.spec.ts`
   regression follow-up, LTR/RTL visual baseline byte-distinction, and the
   live tasks-tab Playwright run all gate the primitive's production
   readiness.
4. **RLS pre-existing fail** (`D-54-04`: `countries` row stuck in
   `sensitiveTables` from Phase 03/04 vintage) plus the
   `useStakeholderInteractionMutations` shim (last v6.2 holdover) are
   security + type-correctness gaps that v7.0's RBAC layer would inherit
   and amplify.

**Exploration decision (user, 2026-05-17):** carryover sweep first, then v7.0.
Tier-C cleanup scoped at **full clear** (most aggressive option) — v6.4 ships
with zero Tier-C debt.

## Out of scope for v6.4

- Anything in the v7.0 Intelligence Engine seed
  (`.planning/seeds/v7.0-intelligence-engine.md`). v6.4 stays narrow on
  carryover; v7.0 starts fresh on stable ground.
- Net-new design tokens or direction variants. Tier-C cleanup is mechanical
  swaps, not token-system extension.
- New e2e specs beyond what D-22 (live tasks-tab Playwright) explicitly
  requires.
